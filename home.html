Coffee Roaster' s Cafe Mission Statement

Here at Coffee Roasters Cafe`, our mission is to provide our customers with spectacular customer service and an impeccable cafe` experience by providing a relaxing environment that inspires the nature of the human spirit.

The History Behind It All

Coffee Roasters Cafe` has been providing exceptional service to its passionate coffee loving customers since 1998. Jim Roaster, founder of the Coffee Roasters Cafe`, always had a passion for his coffee and the mind of an entrepreneur. He would spend most of his college days locking himself in the local cafe` studying for hours upon hours. By the end of his senior year he had graduated with a degree in Business and had hoarded many ideas to open his own cafe` that would give locals a more relaxing environment to get their work needs accomplished. He opened up our first location on the South Hill of Spokane, Washington.
