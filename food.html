Sandwiches

All of our sandwiches are freshly made as ordered. For our breakfast lovers, we have croissant breakfast sandwiches with your choice of sausage, ham, or bacon. If it's lunch that you're visiting us for, we offer an assortment of pin inis or, for more traditionally made sandwiches, you can have your choice of white, whole wheat, sour dough, and many other delicious breads.

Pastas

we also offer a wonderful variety of pasta salads that are guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds. Our most popular pasta salad is our Zesty Southwest Pasta Salad. This salad consists of chopped olives, onions, chili powder, red peppers, grilled chicken, and a hint of garlic. A close second to the Zesty Southwest is the Pesto Tortellini Pasta Salad. This savory dish is filled with pesto, tomatoes, olive oil, a pinch of basil, and chopped green beans. If these two pasta salads aren't calling your way, then there's still plenty to choose from such as the Italian, Asian, and classic macaroni pasta salads.

Treats

Experiencing a bad case of the sweet tooth? Coffee Roaster's Cafe has a variety of sweets to satisfy your needs. From rich sweets such as cupcakes, cakepops, and cookies, to the not to rich sweets like scones and short breads.
