Coffee Roaster's Famous Coffee

Our famous Coffee Roaster's coffee is imported right from the region of Hawaii and is of the "Kona" variety. We provide Kona blends with Columbian and Brazilian beans as well to give our customers a sense of relaxation away from there current residence. These blends also come in light, medium, medium-dark, and for the customers who really enjoy the full potential of these beans, the dark blend. Some of our popular requested drinks are the Tropical Frappuccino, Turtle Latte, and the Caramel Cappuccino.

Tea

For our customers that enjoy the herbal brews, we offer a wide variety of teas such as Mint, Pomegranate, Peach, Pumpkin Spice, and many others. Combinations of 2 teas are offered as well. We highly recommend the Lemon Pomegranate mix!

Other Drinks

If the herbal teas and coffee isn't what you're looking for, many of our customers enjoy our Raspberry Cheesecake Freeze, Pumpkin Chai Latte, and Mudslide Shake. Of course we can't forget the classics either, such as Hot Cocoa, Hot Apple Cider, and Italian Sodas.
